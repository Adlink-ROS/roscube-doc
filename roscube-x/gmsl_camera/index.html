Supported model: ROScube RQX-58G series only

RQX-58G is one of the ROScubeX series model, which extends GMSL 2 standard protocol cameras.

GMSL 2 is SerDes protocol which provides high bandwidth and low latency video streaming for high resolution cameras.

Unlike IP camera or other USB UVC standard cameras, GMSL 2 camera provide RAW frame which has not been compressed by certain encoding algothrim, e.g. H.264 or H.265.

Warning

GMSL 2 camera does not support hot-plugging. Please connect the camera before bootup, or ROScube-X can not detect it.
