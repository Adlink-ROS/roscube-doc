How to flash image
##################

Prerequisite
^^^^^^^^^^^^

Before flashing image to ROScube, you should prepare the following items:

* **Host PC** with **Ubuntu 18.04 or 20.04** operating system.
* A good quality micro-usb for connecting to ROScube.
